About this page

Welcome to our interactive website! Dive into the world of smart media choices with our Media Choices Tree, groove to our educational rap song, find your calm with a relaxing meditation, and have fun with an engaging alphabet soup game. Explore and learn how to make healthier media decisions while having fun!

Media Choices Tree

Welcome to The Media Choices Tree! Here, you'll explore different types of media and learn how to make smart choices. Just like a tree needs strong roots and branches to grow, we need good habits and knowledge to use media wisely. Dive in to discover how media affects you and how you can pick the best options for a healthy, happy life!
